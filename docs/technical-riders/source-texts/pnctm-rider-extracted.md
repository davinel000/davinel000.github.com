Technical Rider

PNCTM is a multimedia interactive installation that reanimates historical diaslides using AI and generative tools, creating a reflective dialogue about the impermanence and malleability of memory. The installation features three frame boxes with integrated screens displaying dynamic animations beneath historical slides, enhanced by interactive magnifying lenses and an immersive ambient soundtrack.

Each frame box stands on a small pedestal and consists of a curated archive diaslide overlaid on a compact high-definition screen, with a media player and a magnifying glass allowing individual perception of the visual story. The audiovisual content employs generative AI techniques based on the original diaslide content and their interpretation, along with audio and video processing algorithms. The installation presents a 3-minute audiovisual composition running in a loop in each frame box, with soft sound textures aimed to create an ASMR effect and immerse the visitor.

## Dimensions / Duration

- Each frame box on a stand: 35 x 35 x 120 cm
- Total installation size: 250 x 35 x 120 cm
- Duration of each video: 3 minutes, cyclical
- Packed dimensions: 60 x 40 x 40 cm Eurobox, mass approx. 15 kg including disassembled stands

## Spatial Requirements

- Minimum room size: 350 x 250 cm (approx. 8 m2) to accommodate all frame boxes
- Alternative arrangements for non-standard rooms are possible but subject to discussion
- Preferred space: a separated and enclosed room is highly recommended for an intimate and focused interaction
- Lighting: the room should be completely dark, with low-brightness red artificial light and a dark curtain (e.g. black Molton) at the entrance or another light trap
- Wall colors: winter white (RAL 0009000) or compatible shades that harmonize with the red light
- Environmental conditions: the space should be free from high humidity, dirt, and dust to ensure the operation of 12v non-waterproof electronic equipment connected to a 220v power supply

## Technical Requirements (Provided by Exhibition / Venue)

- Mains power: 220v / 50hz / max 3A, Schuko plug system
- Power extension in dark color with at least 4 plugs
- 3 plugs are needed for 12v 3A power adapters, 1 for optional light source
- Maximum distance of plugs from artwork is 2 m

## Components Provided by the Artist

- 3 photoboxes, each containing:
- MDF-made photo frame
- 5-inch FHD photo preview screen (Lilliput A5)
- Integrated Android media player
- Custom casing, cables, transformer, on/off switch, remote receiver, and mounting board
- Active air cooling
- 3 pre-focused magnifying lenses
- 3 speaker stands
- 2 remote controls for Android media players with batteries
- 3 x 12v 3A adapters
- 3 extension cables for 12v adapters (2 m, 4 m, 7 m)
- Cleaning accessories for glass surfaces

## Sound

The artwork includes a soft sound composition played through small speakers integrated into the HD screens. It requires its own acoustic space to avoid interference from other artworks. Headphones or additional speakers are not suitable. The sound may interfere with nearby silent artworks.

## Lighting

- Red light sources (LED or white with red filter) can be used on location
- Existing light can be adapted subject to prior discussion
- Red wall washers are recommended
- Positioning of light sources should avoid creating strong reflections on the glossy surface of the photoboxes

Studies of Change, Bremen. Photo: Slava Romanov

## Safety of the Artwork On-site

1. The photoboxes will be loosely attached to the stands to allow access to the power switch, while preventing accidental movement outside the exhibition space.
2. The magnifying lenses will be secured to each stand to prevent falling or accidental removal.

## Transport Requirements

1. The artwork will be delivered in a standard plastic Eurobox (60 x 40 x 40 cm) with a lid.
2. The artwork is fragile and must be handled with care. Necessary safety measures for transportation will be provided by the artist.

## Setup / Installation

- Installation time: 8 hours
- The artist will provide on-site installation
- Assistance may be required for preparing the space, such as darkening light sources and enclosing doorways with Molton

## Maintenance

Regular maintenance includes:

1. Daily cleaning of the glass covers and magnifying lenses with provided cleaning accessories.
2. Daily switching on and off (individual power, power on screens).

Detailed instructions on the switching process and troubleshooting common errors are available upon request.

An autostart procedure can be adapted to exhibition requirements.
